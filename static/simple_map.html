Understanding LGAs

Local Government Areas (LGAs) in Victoria are the third tier of government in Australia, after federal and state governments. These areas are responsible for community services, local planning, and building regulations.

Building permits and regulations often vary by LGA, making it important to check the specific requirements for your area before beginning construction.
